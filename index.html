Why pick Camden Market?

Don't Tumble Darling, was first thought up during me and Simon's 2nd year of University during a 'deep dicussion' about our guaranteed joint future. Our love for London was truly something to consider while we were deciding where to locate, and the quirkiness and artsy side to Camden was practically impossible to avoid. The diversity of the area along with other draw-in aspects to the area is more than likely to help encourage new customers.
A sense of community is something Simon and I take pride in, the need for comfort and happiness an essential aspect to the creation of our quote-on-quote Hipster Laundromat. Camden is also full of both students and young adults just like us! We are however, well aware of the competition we will be facing due to this location, the market having lots of good food options (not as good as ours mind) along with the other 'trendy' bars and clubs who also have live music. This has motivated us even further to ensure our customers have the best experience they can while with us.

A deep-dive into the creation of our menu:
{Our collective journey}

It was hard work but we made it

First, we both had to think of what our desired customer would want to eat, as well as something that we would enjoy cooking. This lead to quite a few different options, many of which that were unfeasible as a start-up business. One of the things that we felt most passionate about was the menu being accessable to all, therefore we made the decision to make the majority of the food menu vegetarian. The inclusion of meat options within both the panini selection and one of the burgers, being enough to satisfy those who do not want so much green. One of my favourite things on the 'Day Menu' being the "All Day Garden Breakfast" as well as Simon's favourite being the "Halloumi Burger".

The cocktail menu on the other hand, was a different story. We needed to stand out. We all know the classics but we felt that we needed to add a twist to make us stand out. This resulted in the creation of our unique and themed cocktail names, everyones favourites reimagined to perfectly fit both the Don't Tumble Darling's vibe and what it has to offer to its loyal customers. The inclusion of cocktails to our menu was simply to create a new and modern atmostphere to the humble laundromat, one that we hope the customer wont have felt before (plus who doesn't love a cheeky drink?)
